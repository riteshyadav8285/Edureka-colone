Let's Begin to Learn!

Hi RITESH,

Before embarking on your learning journey with Acacia, let us walk you through a few important features of your LMS ( Learning Management System), which will guide you on the optimum path to course completion.

Curriculum

This can be seen on the homepage adjacent to the course name. This is a summary of your course content as all your important events - Assessments, quizzes, and submission deadlines will be notified here.

Grade

Your grade is calculated based on timely submissions of Assessments, Quizzes, and DIYs. Make sure you don't miss out on any!

Connect to Adjunct Faculty

For all your technical queries, if you need any assistance, feel free to reach out to your Adjunct Faculty available at the center.

Support

For all your non-technical queries, if you need any assistance, feel free to raise a ticket through your LMS by clicking on the ‘Support’ icon at the bottom right of the page.

Placement

The placement section in your LMS shall act as an assistant to help you create an impressive resume, learn basic skills, job etiquette, and apply for open job opportunities in the industry.

FAQs

Go to the FAQ section in your LMS and look for your query and solution for a quick turnaround.
